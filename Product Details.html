Asafoetida, also known as "hing," is a pungent spice derived from the resin of the Ferula plant. Commonly used in Indian and Middle Eastern cuisine, it adds a distinct flavor to dishes. Asafoetida is known for its strong aroma and is often used as a substitute for garlic and onion in recipes.
